What is WILD?

WILD is a cryptocurrency built with a mission: to protect endangered species and restore natural ecosystems. It creates a transparent, blockchain-based funding stream that allows anyone to support verified conservation projects across the globe.

Powered by decentralized finance (DeFi), WILD gives donors, investors, and environmentalists a way to contribute meaningfully to the planet’s future. By holding and staking WILD, you're part of a community shaping the conservation movement.
